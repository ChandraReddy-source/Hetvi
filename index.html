About Us

Hetvi Fire Services specializes in comprehensive fire protection solutions — supplying ISI-certified extinguishers, installing fire hydrant and alarm systems, conducting safety audits, and offering AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts). With a mission to safeguard lives and property, we aim to deliver reliable, high-quality fire safety services to residential, commercial, and industrial clients.
